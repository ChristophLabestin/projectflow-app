# ProjectFlow - AI powered project and marketing command center

## Product overview
ProjectFlow is a collaborative workspace that combines project planning, flow based ideation, execution, and marketing delivery in one product. Teams can move from idea to launch with AI assistance, structured pipelines, and full visibility across tasks, campaigns, and outcomes.

## Core features (must highlight on landing page)
- Workspaces and multi project dashboards with quick navigation and search
- Flow pipelines with stages (ideation, drafting, review, approval) and conversion to execution
- Task management with priorities, assignees, due dates, subtasks, and checklists
- Issue tracking for bugs and blockers
- Milestones and progress tracking
- Project health score with risks, recommendations, and trend signals
- AI Studio tools: Architect (blueprint), Brainstormer (ideas), RiskScout (risk analysis)
- Social Studio for campaign strategy, posts, approvals, scheduling, and performance planning
- Marketing suite for paid ads and email campaigns
- Drag and drop email builder with reusable templates and variables
- Recipient management with imports and segmentation
- Media library for assets and creative files
- Mind map for visual planning
- Activity log for changes and audit history
- Global search with AI answers across projects, tasks, issues, and flows
- Help center drawer and guided onboarding tours
- Role based access for workspace and project permissions
- Localization (English and German) with light and dark themes

## Core modules and what they do
- Flows: structured ideation pipeline with AI tools, stage gates, and approvals
- Tasks: execution layer for work items, priorities, and timelines
- Issues: defect and blocker tracking tied to projects
- Milestones: checkpoints and delivery goals
- Social Studio: campaign strategy, phases, KPIs, post creation, review, calendar
- Marketing: paid ads campaigns, ad sets, and email campaigns
- Email Builder: drag and drop layout editor with blocks, templates, and drafts
- Recipients: CSV import, fields, and audience lists for email outreach
- Mind Map: visual planning linked to flows
- Activity: timeline of edits, comments, and system events

## AI capabilities
- AI Studio for blueprints, brainstorming, and risk analysis
- AI generated social captions and content planning
- AI assisted search for workspace knowledge and answers
- Usage tracking with monthly quota awareness

## Social Studio highlights
- Campaign strategy builder with phases, KPIs, and channel mix
- Post creation for multiple platforms with previews
- Approval workflows and review actions
- Scheduling with calendar and unscheduled queues
- Asset and preset management for reusable captions and hashtags
- Multi platform fan out to turn one idea into many drafts

## Marketing suite highlights
- Paid ads campaigns with objectives, budgets, and ad sets
- Email campaign creation with templates and drag and drop blocks
- Recipient lists and CSV imports for fast audience setup

## Collaboration and governance
- Project roles (owner, editor, viewer) with scoped capabilities
- Workspace roles (owner, admin, member, guest)
- Comments, activity log, and audit trail

## Ideal audiences
- Product and engineering teams shipping complex projects
- Marketing teams running social and paid campaigns
- Agencies managing multiple clients and deliverables
- Founders and operators who need one workspace from idea to launch
